FAQ

What is the best time to visit India?

The best time to visit India is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant across most regions.

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Yes, most foreign nationals need a visa to visit India. The process can usually be done online through the e-Visa system.

What languages are spoken in India?

India has 22 official languages. Hindi and English are widely spoken, along with regional languages like Tamil, Bengali, and Punjabi.
